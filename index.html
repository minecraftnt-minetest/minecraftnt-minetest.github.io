Minecraftn't is an attempt to recreate Minecraft inf-20100630-2, retroactively referred to as Alpha 1.0.0, as faithfully as possible using the Minetest voxel-based game engine.

Reexperience the classic Minecraft experience in a different, yet familiar way. Take advantage of the various graphical features not found in the original Minecraft Alpha (Smooth Lightning, Dynamic Shadows*, Waving vegetation) and a general better performance than Minecraft Alpha.

The Minetest engine is highly customizable and contains a builtin Lua API for modding, allowing you to quickly and painlessly make mods for Minecraftn't.

What are you waiting for? Download today! Minecraftn't is available for anything that Minetest supports (That is Windows, Linux, Android and macOS*).
